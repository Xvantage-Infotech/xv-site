1. Introduction

Welcome to Nandanvan Farm! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data while using our app.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect certain information to provide better services, including:

  • Booking details (name, contact information, and reservation details).
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party services).
  • Device and usage data to improve user experience.

3. Permissions We Require

Nandanvan Farm App may require the following permissions:

  • Location: To provide directions and navigation assistance.
  • Camera: To scan QR codes for bookings and check-ins.
  • Storage: To save booking confirmations.
  • Internet Access: To update farm availability and process reservations.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to enhance your experience, manage reservations, and ensure a smooth visit to Nandanvan Farm.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to ensure your data remains private. No personal information is shared with third parties except for processing payments.

6. Cancellation & Refund Policy

At Nandanvan Farm, we understand that plans can change. Our flexible cancellation policy ensures a hassle-free experience while maintaining fairness for all guests.

Cancellation Time Cancellation Charges
8 days or more before check-in Free cancellation (full refund to Green Cash Wallet)
7 days before check-in 25% of booking amount deducted
6 days before check-in 30% of booking amount deducted
5 days before check-in 40% of booking amount deducted
4 days before check-in 50% of booking amount deducted
3 days before check-in 60% of booking amount deducted
Less than 48 hours 100% (no refund)

Instant Cancellation: Bookings canceled within 5 minutes of confirmation are eligible for a full refund (excluding convenience fees). After 5 minutes, the standard cancellation policy applies.

Refund Process: Refunds are processed within 24 working hours and credited to your Green Cash Wallet for future bookings. Convenience fees, if applicable, will be deducted before processing.
