Driving Licence & Vehicle Info

A Driving Licence & Vehicle Info, also known as a driver's license or driver's permit, is an official document issued by a government authority that grants the holder the legal right to drive a motor vehicle on public roads. It serves as proof that the individual has passed the necessary tests and met the requirements to operate a vehicle safely.

Using the mobile application, vehicle owners can access a wide range of information related to their vehicles. Some of the details that can be obtained include vehicle registration status, owner details, and fitness certificate details.
