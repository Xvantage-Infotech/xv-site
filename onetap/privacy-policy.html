Welcome to One Tap App! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data while using our app.

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information. However, we may access certain device features locally to enable app functionalities:

  • QR scanner (processed locally, not stored).
  • Internet & GPS speed (measured in real-time, not stored).
  • Battery health & charging status (retrieved in real-time).
  • Room temperature (if supported by the device).
  • Clipboard manager (no external storage).
  • Password generator (passwords are not stored or shared).
  • Number to Word Conversion: Converts numerical inputs into words for better readability. This process is done locally and does not store or share data.

  • Internet Access: Required for URL shortening and fetching internet speed.

All data processing happens locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share personal data.
