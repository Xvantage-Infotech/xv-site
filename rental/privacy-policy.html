Rental App Features & Services

Our Rental App helps users find and book properties for short-term or long-term stays with ease. Whether you are looking for a cozy apartment, a fully-furnished house, or a shared PG, our app provides comprehensive property information and booking options.

Key Features of our Rental App:

  • Search by Rent Type: Daily, Weekly, Monthly
  • Property Categories: Apartment, House, PG, Villas
  • High-Quality Images & Virtual Tours
  • Pricing & Availability
  • Property Reviews & Ratings
  • Nearby Amenities Information
  • Favorites & Comparison Options
  • User-Friendly Interface with Filter Options

Rent Types Covered:

  • Daily Rent: Book properties for a few days or short stays.
  • Weekly Rent: Ideal for business trips or short-term projects.
  • Monthly Rent: Long-term stays with flexible rental agreements.
  • PG & Shared Rentals: Affordable shared accommodations with amenities.
  • Villas & Premium Homes: Luxury options for special stays.
